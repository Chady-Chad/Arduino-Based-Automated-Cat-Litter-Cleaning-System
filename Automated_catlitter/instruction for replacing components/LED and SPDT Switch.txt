1. When replacing switch/es, please ensure that when soldering wires to the pins of the SPDT switch, 
you have to solder them to the common pin and the normal open pin.

2. For LEDs, always attach a 1k resistor in series. Also, connect the ground of the LED to the Arduino ground, 
whilst the positive (anode) pin of the LED is attached to either pin 7 or pin 13.
